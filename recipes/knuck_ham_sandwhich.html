Knuckle-Ham Sandwich

The Backstory You Never Asked For

Growing up in the small town of Knuckles Illinois, I remember knuckle cousine being an essential part of our lifestyle. We ate nothing else but various knuckle dishes, so there was no choice but to get extremely creative with recipes. As a community, there is a common known practice called "Bulls Hitting", (I know, weird name, and I have no idea where it comes from!) for those days when creativity and innovation is lacking.

Bulls Hitting is the tradition of inviting your friends and family over for a lavish knuckle dinner, and making it up as you go. Really. You write random food items on a sheet with whatever measurements you deem appropriate, and wing it! You either end up with a horrible dish and priceless memories from pranking your guests, or you make a great dish. With that being said, here is one dish my sister and I came up with through this great tradition. If I had one piece of advice for you going into this, it would be to trust the process. You will think this recipe is ridiculous, but I promise it will rock your socks off!


What you'll need:

  • 1 pair of knuckles (they must mean 'seroius business'
  • 2 Slices of bread
  • A slice of ham, or your preffered protein substitute
  • Your go-to favorite sandwich condiments!
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 large appetite fordanger

The prep time for this knuck-ham sandwich is 2 minutes, but digestion time will take 1 hour. Lets get started!

  1. Have all of your ingredients measured and ready.
  2. Place your slices of bread on a plate AGGRESSIVELY.
  3. Place your chosen ingredients on either bread slice, but keep balance in mind.
  4. Carefully place 1 pair of knuckles on top of the bottom bread slice.
  5. Place your slice of ham, or substitute, on top of the knuckles.
  6. Using the other slice of bread, close your sandwich.
  7. Ponder the sandwich for 1 hour.

Your Knuck-Ham Sandwich is ready for action! Enjoy!
